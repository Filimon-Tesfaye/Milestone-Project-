Introduction & History

This page summarizes the early history of HTTP and key milestones.

Milestones

  1. 1991 — HTTP/0.9 introduced by Tim Berners-Lee.
  2. 1996–1997 — HTTP/1.0 and HTTP/1.1 standardized with headers and persistent connections.
  3. 2015 — HTTP/2 (binary framing, multiplexing).
  4. ~2020s — HTTP/3 using QUIC adoption grows.

Quick comparison

VersionTransportMain improvement
HTTP/0.9TCPSimple GET, no headers
HTTP/1.1TCPPersistent connections, chunked encoding
HTTP/2TCPBinary framing, multiplexing
HTTP/3QUIC (UDP)Connection migration, lower latency
